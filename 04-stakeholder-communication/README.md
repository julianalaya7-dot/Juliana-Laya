## 📋 Context & Objective

**SafeRetail Group** is a large European retail company that has experienced a significant
increase in phishing and email-based threats over the past six months.
While no major breach has occurred, several incidents nearly resulted in
financial loss and reputational damage.

**The challenge:** The executive board has limited understanding of cybersecurity risks
and needs to be convinced of the need to invest in an
**Advanced Email Threat Detection System.**

**The ask:** Approve a **€30,000 pilot for Q3 2024.**
